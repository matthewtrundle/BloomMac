What Self-Compassion Is NOT

🫥

Not Self-Pity

It's acknowledgment, not wallowing in your struggles

💪

Not Weakness

It takes courage to be kind to yourself when you're struggling

⚖️

Not Letting Go

You can be compassionate AND responsible

🌟

Not Selfish

Your wellbeing directly impacts your ability to care for others

Three Essential Components

  • 💗
    Self-Kindness: Actively comforting yourself when struggling
  • 🤝
    Common Humanity: You're not alone—85% of mothers feel overwhelmed
  • 🌊
    Mindfulness: Observing thoughts without drowning in them

Research shows mothers practicing self-kindness have 40% lower depression symptoms

A Simple Practice for Difficult Moments

Place Your Hand on Your Heart

"This is a moment of struggle"
That's mindfulness—recognizing what's happening
"Struggle is part of being human"
That's common humanity—you're not alone
"May I be kind to myself"
That's self-kindness—the heart of compassion

Less than a minute that can shift everything

Make It Your Own

The exact words don't matter. What matters is the intention to treat yourself with care.

"This is really hard right now"
"Other moms feel this way too"
"May I give myself the compassion I need"

Less than a minute, but it can change everything
