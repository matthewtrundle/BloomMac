Why "Good Enough" Is Actually Best

Perfect attunement actually hinders development. Babies need imperfect mothers because that's how they learn:

1

Frustration Tolerance

When you don't respond instantly, babies learn they can handle waiting and develop self-soothing skills

2

Repair Is Possible

When things go wrong and you reconnect, babies learn that disruptions can be healed and relationships can be mended

3

Separate Beings

Your imperfections help them understand they are individuals with their own agency and capabilities
