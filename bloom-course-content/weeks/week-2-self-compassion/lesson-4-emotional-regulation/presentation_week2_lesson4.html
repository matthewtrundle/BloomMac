When a Big Emotion Hits:

  1. Name it: "Rage is here"
  2. Find it in your body
  3. Breathe into that space
  4. Let it move through you
  5. Trust it will pass

"You're not your emotions. You're the awareness experiencing them."

The Power of Co-Regulation

We're not meant to regulate alone.

Our nervous systems are designed to co-regulate with others. When isolation makes everything harder, you can co-regulate with:

  • A pet (their steady breathing helps)
  • A guided meditation (borrowed calm)
  • A tree (lean against it)
  • A soothing voice recording

You're not weak for needing others. You're human.
