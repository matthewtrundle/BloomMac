Your Quick Relief Menu

For immediate relief in 30 seconds to 3 minutes

When Anxious/Panicked

  • 4-7-8 breathing
  • Ice cube on wrist
  • Name 5 blue things
  • Shake for 30 seconds

When Rageful/Frustrated

  • Push wall hard (10 sec)
  • Scream into pillow
  • Rip up paper
  • Stomp feet 20 times

When Numb/Disconnected

  • Smell peppermint oil
  • Cold water on face
  • 10 jumping jacks
  • Call for a voice

When Overwhelmed/Crying

  • Hand on heart
  • Wrap in blanket
  • Rock yourself
  • Let tears flow

Write these on index cards. Put them everywhere.

The Secret to Success

Practice when calm,
so it's automatic
when you're not.

Like fire drills—practice when there's no fire
so your body knows what to do when there is one.

Even 2 minutes daily builds the neural pathways you need.

Remember This Truth

Your coping toolkit isn't about becoming a different person.

It's about supporting the person you are through an incredibly challenging time.

Some days you'll use every tool.
Some days you'll forget they exist.
Both are okay.

The toolkit is there when you need it, judgment-free and ready.
