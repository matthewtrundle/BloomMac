The Basic Exercise
(Stanley Rosenberg)

Resets your nervous system through eye movements:

  1. Lie on your back comfortably
  2. Interlace fingers behind your head
  3. Look right with eyes only (not head) for 30 seconds
  4. Return eyes to center
  5. Look left with eyes only for 30 seconds
  6. Return to center and rest

This simple exercise can shift your entire nervous system state
