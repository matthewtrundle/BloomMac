Vagus Nerve Hacks

Direct stimulation techniques:

❄️ Cold Water Therapy

Splash cold water on face or hold ice on temples. Activates dive response, immediately slows heart rate.

🎵 Humming & Singing

Hum lullabies or sing to baby. Vibrations stimulate the vagus nerve through your throat.

🌙 Gentle Neck Stretches

Slow head turns and rolls. The vagus nerve runs through your neck—stretching releases tension.

🤗 Loving Touch

Hand on heart or self-hug. Touch releases oxytocin, your body's natural anti-anxiety hormone.

Movement for Anxiety Release

Anxiety is energy that needs somewhere to go—discharge it through movement

🤸 Shaking Practice

Shake your whole body vigorously for 30 seconds. Stop and notice the calm. Animals do this naturally after stress.

💪 Wall Push-Ups

Push hard against wall for 10 seconds. Gives your fight-or-flight energy something productive to do.

🧘 Legs Up the Wall

Lie with legs up wall for 5-10 minutes. This posture tells your nervous system "no running needed here."

💃 Dance It Out

One song, move however feels good. Movement with music is double nervous system medicine.

The Basic Exercise
(Stanley Rosenberg)

Resets your nervous system through eye movements:

  1. Lie on your back comfortably
  2. Interlace fingers behind your head
  3. Look right with eyes only (not head) for 30 seconds
  4. Return eyes to center
  5. Look left with eyes only for 30 seconds
  6. Return to center and rest

This simple exercise can shift your entire nervous system state
