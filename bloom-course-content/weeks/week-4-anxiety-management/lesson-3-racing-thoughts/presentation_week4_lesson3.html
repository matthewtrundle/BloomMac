The Thought Record

CBT simplified for exhausted mothers:

1. The thought: "I'm failing my baby"
2. The feeling: Panic, shame
3. Evidence for: Baby cried for an hour
4. Evidence against: Baby is healthy, gaining weight, mostly content
5. Balanced thought: I'm learning to understand my baby's needs
