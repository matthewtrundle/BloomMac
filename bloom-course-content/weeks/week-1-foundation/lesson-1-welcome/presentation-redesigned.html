Ancient Understanding

Cultural Wisdom & Your Journey

Sacred Traditions

Many cultures have always recognized the sacred nature of postpartum:

  • China: "Sitting the month" - protected healing time
  • Latin America: "La cuarentena" - 40 days of care
  • India: Confinement period with family support

Modern Truth

These aren't outdated traditions. They're wisdom we've lost in our rush to "bounce back."

Your body is healing. Your brain is rewiring. Your identity is shifting. This takes time, and that time is sacred, not selfish.
