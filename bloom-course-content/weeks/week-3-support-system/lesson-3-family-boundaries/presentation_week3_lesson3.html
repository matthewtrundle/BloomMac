The Boundary Setting Formula

Appreciation

"I'm so grateful you want to be involved..."

Specific Boundary

"I need everyone to text before visiting..."

Reason (Optional)

"This helps me manage baby's schedule..."

Alternative/Compromise

"How about we set up regular Sunday visits?"

Boundaries presented with love are still boundaries

Scripts That Actually Work

For Unexpected Visits:

"Mom, I love that you want to see the baby. I need you to text before coming over so I can let you know if it's a good time. How about we plan your visits in advance?"

For Unsolicited Advice:

"I appreciate your experience. Right now, I'm following our pediatrician's guidance. I'll definitely ask if I need suggestions."

For Social Media:

"We're not sharing baby photos online yet. We'd love to text you photos directly that you can enjoy privately."

Building Sustainable Relationships

Boundaries aren't just about saying no:

  • Define Helpful Help: "Bring dinner and eat with us"
  • Create Roles: Grandpa = evening walks
  • Schedule Connection: "Wednesdays are Grandma days"
  • United Front: Partner sets boundaries with their family
  • Model Don't Lecture: Show how to soothe baby

Structure creates freedom for everyone
