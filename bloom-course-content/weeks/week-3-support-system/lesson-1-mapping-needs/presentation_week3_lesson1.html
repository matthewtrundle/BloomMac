Scripts That Actually Work

For Practical Help:

"I'm struggling with laundry. Would you be willing to come fold clothes with me Thursday afternoon while we chat?"

For Emotional Support:

"I'm having a hard time and could use someone to listen. No advice needed, just a caring ear. Do you have 20 minutes?"

For Information:

"You mentioned you dealt with sleep issues. Could I ask you a few questions about what worked?"
