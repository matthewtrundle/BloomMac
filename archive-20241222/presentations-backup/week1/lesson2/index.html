Sleep Architecture Changes

Your sleep patterns have fundamentally changed. This isn't just about less sleep - it's about different sleep.

What's Happening:

  • Less deep sleep (protective mechanism)
  • More light sleep (responsive to baby)
  • Fragmented REM cycles
  • Hypervigilance even while "sleeping"

This is temporary. Your brain will recalibrate, but it takes time.
