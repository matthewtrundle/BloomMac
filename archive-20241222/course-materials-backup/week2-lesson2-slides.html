Breaking the Comparison Addiction

The Comparison Trap
You're comparing your behind-the-scenes to everyone's highlight reel
3.7
Hours daily on social media
147
Comparisons made per day
89%
Feel worse after comparing
Digital Detox Steps
  1. Unfollow accounts that make you feel inadequate
  2. Set time limits - 30 min max per day
  3. No scroll zones - bedroom, nursing, meals
  4. Replace with real connection - text a friend instead
  5. Follow real mothers who show the messy truth
Comparison Antidotes
When you catch yourself comparing:

1. Name it: "I'm comparing again"
2. Get curious: "What am I really needing?"
3. Celebrate someone: Turn envy into appreciation
4. List your wins: What went right today?
5. Remember: You're seeing their Act 3, not Acts 1 & 2
Your Only Competition
Yesterday's version of you.
Are you kinder to yourself than yesterday?
That's the only metric that matters.
